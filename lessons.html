Lessons

I offer free online trial sessions (lessons & consultations) before starting the first one. If you don’t think we are compatible, that is no problem. It’s important you feel happy before committing yourself.

Type 1: KS3 & GCSE (KS4)

This is the main course for complete beginners to someone with more knowledge. I will build up a good foundation for speaking, reading, listening and writing, which will lead to GCSE exam preparation. If you require any SEN support, please let me know. We can figure out the best way for learning.

For students who are not sure which tier exam they should take, I could evaluate the current level (GCSE 1-9) and give advice. You have no obligation to take any further lessons with me.

Type 2: Beyond GCSE level

This is the course for students who wants to learn further. With higher level grammar and vocabulary, more reading of Japanese stories and news articles, etc.

Type 3: Travel conversation and other requirements

If you have a specific need such as travel conversation skills before going to Japan, improving speaking or reading stories in Japanese, I can meet your requirements.
